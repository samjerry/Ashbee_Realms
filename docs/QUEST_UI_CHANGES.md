## User Flow

### Before
1. Open Quest Log
2. Click "Available" tab
3. Browse available quests
4. Click "Accept Quest"
5. Quest moves to Active tab

### After
1. **Option A - NPC:**
   - Explore world and find NPC
   - Click on NPC
   - View NPC dialogue and available quests
   - Select quest to view details
   - Click "Accept Quest"
   - Quest appears in Quest Log

2. **Option B - Quest Board:**
   - Travel to a town
   - Find and click Quest Board
   - Browse available quests
   - Select quest to view details
   - Click "Accept Quest"
   - Quest appears in Quest Log

## Benefits

1. **More Immersive** - Players must explore the world to find quests
2. **Better Storytelling** - Quests tied to NPCs create narrative connections
3. **Clearer UI** - Quest Log is simpler, focused on tracking progress
4. **Discoverable** - Quest Boards in towns provide clear quest hubs
5. **Scalable** - Foundation for NPC interaction system and quest chains
